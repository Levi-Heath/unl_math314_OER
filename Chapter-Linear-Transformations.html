    Chapter 6 Linear Transformations

    Objectives

    • Interpret a matrix as a linear transformation from \(\mathbb{R}^n\) to \(\mathbb{R}^m\text{.}\)
    • Produce a matrix representation of a linear transformation given its action on the standard unit vectors.
    • Identify some basic geometric transformations of the plane.
    • Discuss the kernel and image of the transformation geometrically and in terms of nullity and rank of the matrix.
    • Give examples of linear transformations that are invertible or not invertible.
    • Interpret a matrix product as a composition of linear transformations.
