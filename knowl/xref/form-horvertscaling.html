Formula 6.4.1. Horizontal and Vertical Scaling.

A matrix transformation induced by
\begin{equation} M_v=\begin{bmatrix} 1 \amp 0\\ 0 \amp k \end{bmatrix},\tag{6.4.1} \end{equation}
where (\(k\gt 0\)), scales objects in the plane vertically by a factor of \(k\text{.}\) A matrix transformation induced by
\begin{equation} M_h=\begin{bmatrix} k \amp 0\\ 0 \amp 1 \end{bmatrix},\tag{6.4.2} \end{equation}
where (\(k\gt 0\)), scales objects in the plane horizontally by a factor of \(k\text{.}\)
