Formula 6.4.5. Counterclockwise Rotation.

A matrix transformation induced by
\begin{equation} M_{\theta}=\begin{bmatrix} \cos\theta \amp -\sin\theta\\ \sin\theta \amp \cos\theta \end{bmatrix}\tag{6.4.5} \end{equation}
rotates the plane counterclockwise through angle \(\theta\) about the origin.
