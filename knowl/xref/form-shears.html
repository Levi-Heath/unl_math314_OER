Formula 6.4.2. Horizontal and Vertical Shears.

A matrix transformation induced by
\begin{equation} M_{hs}=\begin{bmatrix} 1 \amp k\\ 0 \amp 1 \end{bmatrix}\tag{6.4.3} \end{equation}
shears the plane horizontally. A matrix transformation induced by
\begin{equation} M_{vs}=\begin{bmatrix} 1 \amp 0\\ k \amp 1 \end{bmatrix}\tag{6.4.4} \end{equation}
shears the plane vertically.
