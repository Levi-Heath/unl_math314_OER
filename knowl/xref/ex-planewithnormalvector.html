Example 1.3.10.

Find an equation of the plane through \(P_{0}(1, -1, 3)\) with \(\mathbf{n} = \begin{bmatrix} 3\\ -1\\ 2 \end{bmatrix}\) as a normal vector. \begin{explanation} Here the equation becomes
\begin{equation*} 3(x - 1) - (y + 1) + 2(z - 3) = 0 \end{equation*}
This simplifies to \(3x - y + 2z = 10\text{.}\)
